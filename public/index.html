Tops (Table & Bench)

Choose edge profile: Rustic

Our rustic finish is sanded to 80grit and we strive to maintain as much of the rough sawn edge in the grain as possible. Its in the courser grain the wax grabs to create a beautiful two-tone rustic finish. A key attribute of the rustic finish is that there may be slight undulations in each board. This does not detract the practicality of the table-top.

Our modern planed finish is achieved by planing the boards as smooth as possible to make them perfectly flat and even-grained. When wax is applied to a planed board the finish is more consistent. A key attribute of the planed finish is that each board is perfectly flat.

Choose table top finish: Wax Polish

Formulated to offer excellent results on kiln-dried pine, Fiddes Supreme Wax Polish contains a unique, environmentally friendly blend of waxes that naturally enhances the beauty of your wood. We advise to maintain once a year & of course, we are happy to advise on this.

Hard Wax Oil gives a highly durable water and stain resistant finish that is child safe when dry. Can also be used outside and has UV protective qualities. It is recommended that outdoor tables are covered during adverse weather and in the winter.

Choose wood colour: Clear Wax

Clear wax is similar to natural raw oak. The finish is semi translucent and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a light grey tone.

Lime wax has a shabby chic look to it. The finish has a pale/white colour and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a chalky white tone.

Grey wash has a contemporary feel to it. The finish has a medium grey colour and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a dark grey tone.

Antique Oak is a Manns branded water based wood dye. This light brown finish is inbetween clear wax and forest brown and is ideal for customers looking for a very natural light brown colour tone.

Forest brown is similar to honey oak. The finish has a golden colour and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a deep rustic orange tone.

Rugger brown is similar to medium oak. The finish has a light brown colour and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a deeper rustic brown tone.

Jacobean is similar to walnut or mahogany but without the red tones. The finish has a brown colour and in the rougher / courser areas of the grain the wax grabs to create a deeper dark brown tone.
